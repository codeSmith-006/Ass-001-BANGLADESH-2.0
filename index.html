The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Ladies and gentlemen, today I stand before you to share a vision—a vision of a future where Bangladesh shines as a beacon of hope, progress, and resilience. I dream of a Bangladesh where technology bridges gaps, enabling education, healthcare, and governance to reach every corner of our nation. Imagine a future where innovation drives us forward, where our youth lead the way with creativity and determination. Sustainability will guide our path. We will embrace renewable energy, protect our environment, and tackle climate change head-on, ensuring that our prosperity does not come at the cost of our planet. This vision isn’t just mine—it’s ours. Together, we will build a Bangladesh where every citizen has a chance to thrive. The journey starts today. Thank you.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
